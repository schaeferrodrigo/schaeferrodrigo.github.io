About Me

Graduation at Mathematics (2011) and master's at Mathematics (2014) from Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro . Currently, PhD student in Mathematics at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya . Member of the UPC Dynamical System Group and DANCE, a network of spanish reseachers in Dynamical Systems. Has experience in Mathematics, focusing on Dynamical Systems, acting on the following subjects: arnold diffusion and scattering map.
