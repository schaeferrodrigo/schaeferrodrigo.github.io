Rodrigo Schaefer

PhD Student in Applied Math

Graduation at Mathematics (2011) and master's at Mathematics (2014) from Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro . Currently, PhD student in Mathematics at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya . Member of the UPC Dynamical System Group . Has experience in Mathematics, focusing on Dynamical Systems, acting on the following subjects: arnold diffusion and scattering map.


Current research: Arnold diffusion using scattering maps in an a priori unstable Hamiltonian system with 3-1/2 dof.
